Beginning ET Shifts: Pre-Shift Tasks

Welcome to the beginning of your ET shift! Before diving into the work, there are four key tasks you'll need to complete at the start of each shift. Let's walk through them.

1.

  • First Step is arriving to the team room on time for your shift
  •  Make sure to head directly to room #[a] when you arrive on-site. This is where you’ll meet up with your team, get the necessary equipment, and get ready for your shift.

2.  

  • Once you're at the Team Room, the next step is to clock in:
  • Use the current time clock either on your cell phone or on the computer located in the team room to punch in, ask your bm on duty if have any questions  

3.

  •  Next step is getting radio  
  • Radios are essential for communication throughout your shift. Here’s how to get set up:  
  • Pick up a radio and test it to ensure it’s working properly.  
  • If you need assistance with testing the radio, reach out to your BM for help.

4.

  •  Next, you’ll need to check in  keys for your shift:  
  • The Student Center keys are stored in a key box.  
  • Always take the keys in and out of the key box yourself—don’t hand them off between shifts.  
  •  Keep track of the keys and be sure not to lose them.
  • Your PIN to open the key box  is the last five digits of your EID  Number followed by #, so keep that in mind when accessing the key box.

Once you’ve completed these four tasks, you’re ready to start working with your team. Here's what comes next:

  • Huddle with your BM: Discuss the shift plans and any important updates.  
  • Complete Room Checks: Make sure rooms are set up and ready for use.  
  •  Begin Setting Rooms: Follow your BM’s instructions to prepare rooms as needed for the day.

Now that you have completed this training module you are all set to begin your shift and ensure the Student Center runs smoothly!

Video #3 Narration

Pipe and Drape setup

“Welcome to the Pipe and Drape setup tutorial. In this video, we’ll walk you through the steps to set up a single section of pipe and drape. Let’s get started!”

Equipment Overview

“First, here’s the equipment you’ll need to set up one section of pipe and drape:”

  • 2 Bases
  • 2 Black Upright Poles
  • 1 Silver Crossbar Pole
  • 4 Black Drapes
  • 2 Base weights

Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

“Now let’s go through the setup process. Follow these steps carefully to ensure a sturdy and professional result.”

  1. “Start by placing the two bases on the ground. These will provide support for the poles.”
  2. “Make sure each base has a piece attached to securely hold the pole in place.”
  3. “Next, take one upright pole and place it onto each base. Ensure that both poles are standing straight up and down—they should not be leaning or tilted.”
  4. “Now, take the silver crossbar and extend it to the required length until it locks into place.”
  5. “Place four black drapes onto the silver crossbar. Be sure to space them evenly for a uniform look.”
  6. “Attach the silver crossbar to the tops of the black upright poles. Make sure the connection is secure.”
  7. “Then, carefully pull up on the top part of the black upright poles to extend them to the desired height.”
  8. “Finally, step back and double-check that the poles are standing straight and at the same height. This ensures that everything is level and secure.”

“And that’s it! You’ve successfully set up a section of pipe and drape. Make sure to check the stability before leaving it in place, and always follow safety guidelines. Thanks for watching, and happy setting up!”
